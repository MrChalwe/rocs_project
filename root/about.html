About ROCS

Promoting inclusive, equitable and quality education for vulnerable children across Zambia.

Who We Are

Reformed Open Community Schools

Reformed Open Community Schools (ROCS) is a national non-profit organisation registered under the Societies Act of Zambia in May 1997. For over 27 years, ROCS has worked to remove systemic barriers that prevent vulnerable children from accessing equitable and quality education.

Our Vision

A society where all children enjoy the right to equitable quality education.

Our Mission

To promote sustained inclusive and equitable access to quality education for vulnerable children.
