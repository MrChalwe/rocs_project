Who We Are

About ROCS

Reformed Open Community Schools

Since 1997, ROCS has been committed to empowering vulnerable children in Zambia through access to quality, inclusive, and equitable education. As an initiative of the Reformed Church in Zambia, we support community schools and Early Childhood Development Centres (ECCDE) across the country.

Our Vision

A society where all children enjoy the right to equitable quality education.

Our Mission

To promote sustained inclusive and equitable access to quality education for vulnerable children.
